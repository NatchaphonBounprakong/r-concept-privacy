This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use rconcept platform (the “Services”, our “Services”).

 

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

 

When you use our Services, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device. Additionally, as you use or browse our Services, we collect information about the individual pages or products that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to Our Services, and information about how you interact with Our Services. We refer to this automatically-collected information as “Device Information”.

 

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

 

- “Cookies” are data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include an anonymous unique identifier. For more information about cookies, and how to disable cookies, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org. You can also configure your browser to reject the use of cookies.

- “Log files” track actions occurring on Our Services, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.

- “Web beacons”, “tags”, and “pixels” are electronic files used to record information about how you browse Our Services.

 

Additionally, when you create a profile or apply for jobs through Our Services, we collect certain information from you, including your name, email address, Google login, Facebook login, resume, and phone number. We refer to this information as “Candidate Information”.

 

When we talk about “Personal Information” in this Privacy Policy, we are talking both about Device Information and Candidate Information.

 

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

 

We use the Candidate Information that we collect generally to provide potential employers information on qualified candidates for their job openings. Additionally, we use this Information to:

- Communicate with you;

- Provide potential employment opportunities; and

- When in line with the preferences you have shared with us, provide you with information or advertising relating to your career or profession.

 

We use the Device Information that we collect to help us screen for potential risk and fraud (in particular, your IP address), and more generally to improve and optimize our Site (for example, by generating analytics about how our customers browse and interact with Our Services, and to assess the success of our marketing and advertising campaigns).
